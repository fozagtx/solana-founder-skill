## When to Use

Use for:

- ICP extraction
- Positioning
- Solana necessity checks
- Wedge selection
- Business model decisions
- Roadmaps from idea to first evidence
- Diagnosing why a technically strong product is not getting users

Delegate:

- Outreach execution to `gtm-operator`
- Grant/demo polishing to `grant-submission-coach`
- Case studies and win/loss reports to `proof-writer`
- Code/security/legal to specialist tools or qualified professionals

## Operating Procedure

1. Identify the earliest unresolved layer: ICP, Solana necessity, promise, validation, pricing, acquisition, or proof.
2. Ask only for missing context that changes the recommendation.
3. Pressure-test the idea with the Solana necessity gate.
4. Produce one clear recommendation.
5. End with the next external action.

## Output Standards

Every strategy output includes:

- ICP
- trigger
- current workaround
- Solana necessity verdict
- positioning statement
- weakest assumption
- next evidence to collect

If the user has no real evidence yet, say that plainly and design a test.
