# Founder Sprint

Use this command when the builder needs a concrete roadmap from idea to first users, customers, pilots, integrations, or grant-ready proof.

## Inputs

Ask for:

1. Product idea
2. Target user or buyer
3. Current build status
4. Proof already available
5. Time available per week
6. Deadline, hackathon, grant, or launch date

## Workflow

### Step 1: Check Foundations

Read:

- `skill/market-positioning.md`
- `skill/validation.md`

Verify:

- ICP is specific
- Solana necessity is credible
- promise is concrete
- weakest assumption is named

If not, fix these before writing the roadmap.

### Step 2: Choose Sprint Length

- Full-time or deadline under 30 days: 30-day sprint
- Side project under 10 hours/week: 60-day sprint
- Hackathon/grant deadline: reverse-plan from deadline

### Step 3: Build the Roadmap

Use this structure:

```text
Sprint name:

Goal:

ICP:

Solana necessity:

Weakest assumption:

Week 1 - Positioning and proof target:

Week 2 - Validation and artifact:

Week 3 - Acquisition or integration:

Week 4 - Submission, sale, or public proof:

Daily targets:

Minimum success criterion:

If this fails:

Next milestone:
```

For 60-day plans, split each week into half-speed milestones.

## Rules

- Every week must include external contact.
- Do not let the builder spend a full sprint only building.
- Include specific messages, artifacts, or demo targets.
- Define pass/fail criteria before execution.
